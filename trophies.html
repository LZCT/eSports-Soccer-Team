Brazilian Cup

The club's first title came after a difficult match against E.C. Falcons. It was Gabriel Junior's goal in the 87th minute that decided the game and gave the title to the warriors.

2020

Libertadores

After the club's first title, the team disenchanted and won the long-awaited Libertadores Cup in the same year, with a masterful 4x0 against Big Clowns.

2020

Fronteriza Cup

At the beginning of 2021, the club was invited to the Copa Fronteriza, a pre-season tournament of great importance, beating White Magics 2x1 in the final.

2021

Brazilian Championship

With 29 victories in 38 rounds, the warriors confirmed their favoritism and won their first Brazilian championship with a record campaign in points.
