Our Story


The Black Warriors was founded in 2019 as an esports team. In the same year, the club joined several organizations, the main ones being the EFA (Electronic Football Association) and the VPL (Virtual Pro League). In its first year competing, the club managed to fight with some teams already established in the scenario, but did not win any titles. The biggest achievement was staying among the top in most championships. It was the following year that the club disenchanted and won its first title, the Brazilian Cup. In the same year, the club won the much disputed Libertadores Cup.

A year later, the team repeated the same feat, winning two tournaments in one year, the Fronteriza Cup and the Brazilian championship.

In 2022, the team remains in good shape and aims to win at least one title this season.

This Season in Numbers
